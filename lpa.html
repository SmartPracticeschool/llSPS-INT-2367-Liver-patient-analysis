LIVER DISEASE PREDICTOR

Liver diseases have produced a data such as metabolomics analyses, electronic health records, and report including patient medical information, and disorders. However, these data must be analyzed and integrated if they are to produce models about physiological mechanisms of pathogenesis. We use machine learning based on classifier for big datasets in the fields of liver to Predict and therapeutic discovery. A dataset was developed with twenty three attributes that include the records of 520 patients . Support Vector Machine (SVM) for the prediction of liver diseases. The performance of these classifier techniques are evaluated with accuracy, sensitivity, specificity.
